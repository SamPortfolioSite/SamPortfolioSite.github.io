About Me

My name is Sam Aldeguer and I am a fourth-year honors student at the University of Kansas studying Computer Science with a minor in Business. My love for computer science began in elementary school when I would play games on my family's computer and became curious about what went on behind the scenes. Fast forward to now, I worked at Garmin as a Software Engineering Intern in the Fitness division and am currently looking for full-time Software Engineering positions. I enjoy both front-end and back-end development, as I have strengths in C++, C, Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and SQL. I am the President of Upsilon Pi Epsilon, a Computer Science Honor Society; a member of Tau Beta Pi, an Engineering Honor Society; and a Pledge Marshal of Zeta Theta Tau, a professional engineering fraternity. Through my current and prior involvements, I have gained immense leadership, communication, and organizational skills that I hope to utilize in my future career.
